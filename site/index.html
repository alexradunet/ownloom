The idea

Your life is not a pile of data. It is a fabric being woven.

Ownloom starts from a simple belief: your personal context should be yours. Notes, reminders, conversations, routines, files, and devices should not be scattered across opaque services. They should form a private fabric that helps you remember, plan, and act.

The avatar

A living loom that evolves with your system.

The Ownloom avatar begins as a simple frame with a few loose threads. As you connect memory, planner, messages, automations, and devices, it grows into a living loom: a calm visual language for what your agents know, what they are doing, and what needs your attention.
